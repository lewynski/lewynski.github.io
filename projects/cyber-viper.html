> BACKGROUND LORE

In the neon-drenched sectors of the central mainframe, rogue data fragments threaten to crash the entire system. You have been deployed as the CYBER VIPER—an autonomous counter-intrusion program.

Your objective is to consume the glowing Corrupted Cores to stabilize the grid. However, every core you absorb increases your program's mass. The system will overclock, forcing you to navigate at terrifying, escalating speeds.

/// DIRECTIVES ///

Use WASD or ARROWS to steer.
